    content: "<p>Warrants may be classified as:</p>\n<ul>\n<li><strong>Bailable Warrant:</strong> The officer executing the warrant must release the arrested person on bail upon their furnishing security. This is the less severe type.</li>\n<li><strong>Non-Bailable Warrant:</strong> The arrested person must be produced before the court — no bail can be granted at the point of arrest by the police.
